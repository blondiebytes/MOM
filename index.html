MOM: Mind Over Matter

Brainstorm and discover new ideas with Alexa.

Coming up with ideas is hard. That's why we created Mind Over Matter - or what we like to call MOM. Now, you have something to bounce ideas off of and together, you can come up with an idea that is truly unique. Start the MOM skill by saying "Alexa, Start Mind Over Matter." Then, state your assignment (report, project, etc) or the subject you are interested in. Alexa will give you readily available topics to research into and help you discover what you really want to know. At the end of your session, be sure to say "Review My Brainstorm" so you can see how far you've come. Below are the results of your most recent brainstorms.
